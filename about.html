North Texas Arms

Who We Are

ntxarms is a firearms dealer serving the North Texas community. We specialize in firearm transfers, helping customers receive firearms purchased online with professionalism and expertise.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing quality service to responsible gun owners. Our focus is on making the transfer process smooth, compliant, and straightforward.

What We Offer

  • FFL transfer services for online and private purchases
  • Competitive pricing with discounts for Texas LTC holders
  • Knowledgeable staff to guide you through the process
  • Convenient pickup location in Blue Ridge, TX

Our Values

  • Compliance: Strict adherence to all federal and state firearms regulations
  • Service: Responsive communication and professional handling of all transfers
  • Community: Supporting responsible gun ownership in North Texas
  • Integrity: Honest, transparent pricing and processes
